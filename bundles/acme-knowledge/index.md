# Acme Knowledge Base

Authoritative, agent-consumable knowledge for Acme (a fictional B2B SaaS company).
Organised by **knowledge domain**. **Sensitivity** is handled by splitting into
separate bundles/repos — this bundle is `internal`; see [data-classification](/policies/data-classification).

## Directories
- [Glossary](/glossary) — ubiquitous business language (the "words")
- [Metrics](/metrics) — canonical metric definitions (the "numbers")
- [Data](/data) — data catalog: datasets & tables (the "where")
- [Systems](/systems) — services & APIs (the "how it's produced")
- [Runbooks](/runbooks) — operational procedures (the "when it breaks")
- [Playbooks](/playbooks) — repeatable workflows (the "how we do X")
- [Teams](/teams) — ownership & contact (the "who")
- [Decisions](/decisions) — decision records / ADRs (the "why")
- [Policies](/policies) — standards & governance (the "rules")
