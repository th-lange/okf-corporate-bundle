# MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)

Total predictable subscription revenue normalised to a monthly figure. One-off charges
(setup fees, usage overages) are **excluded**.

- Canonical computation: [MRR metric](/metrics/monthly-recurring-revenue).
- Why a single definition exists: [ADR 0001](/decisions/0001-mrr-single-source-of-truth).
